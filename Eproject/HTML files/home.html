About Club

An Idea to establish a sports complex was conceived by the then Administrator DHA, Brig Khalid Masood Ahmed Tirmizi as envisioned by Mr. M. Moin Khan, former Captain, Pakistan Cricket Team. The project was favored by the then President Executive Board, Commander 5 Corps, Lieutenant General Shahid Iqbal. Present location was considered feasible by all means including safety and security of visiting members, their children and academy students. On completion of phase I of the club, it was inaugurated by then Administrator, DHA Brig Aamer Raza Qureshi. The sports complex was unanimously named as "DHA Sports Club (Moin Khan Academy)" by its Board.

Sports Facilities

Like any other sport, cricket players need to follow certain strength and conditioning programs. In our Cricket Academy, we enhance enthusiasm, sharpen their skills, improve mental alertness, fitness development, sportsmanship, and humbleness amongst the youth to become true representatives of the country and achieve the desired goals. We have a team of qualified, experienced, and professional coaches. We are proud of our Academy, which is one of the best Cricket Academies in the world.
